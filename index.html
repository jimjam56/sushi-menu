–History–

Yan San Sushi is a local restaurant that was started in February of 2013. We are happy to finally be in business and bring you the best meal experience possible! To be completely honest though, we have yet to sell a single meal…and we still don't have a store…but our hopes and aspirations are high! Also, if you know anyone that can make sushi, please have them contact us, for our sou chef is currently on leave. Ok. We just don't have one yet. That's ok though, it can't be that hard to make sushi! Until we are able to find a permanent location, you can find us downtown, making tasty meals out of the trunk of a gold Nissan Sentra. Hope to see you soon!
